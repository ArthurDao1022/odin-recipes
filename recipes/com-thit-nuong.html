Com Thit Nuong

Description

One of the famous dishes dedicated from Vietnamese culture: Com Thit Nuong.

You either come here to eat Pho, or Com Thit Nuong. There is no both!

Ingredients

  • Grilled Pork
  • Rice
  • Fish Sauce
  • Chopped Onions
  • Radish
  • Lettuce
  • Fried Egg

Steps

  1. Grill the Pork
  2. Cook Rice
  3. Cut Onions and Lettuce
  4. Fry an egg | Sunny side up or preference towards Customer
  5. Place each item in a special corner and not mixed all around
