Salt and Pepper Tofu

Description

This is a fried stiryfry asian dish that is famously known as: "Salt and Pepper Tofu Stirfry."

The best Salt and Pepper Tofu is located at Pho 99, in Brooklyn Park, Minnesota.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Spices
  • Corn (Optional)

Steps

  1. Mix tofu with Salt and Pepper.
  2. Submerge the tofu into water.
  3. Stirfry it with Salt and Pepper
  4. Heat up the Corn
  5. Place the Tofu on the plate alongside with chopped green onions and corn
